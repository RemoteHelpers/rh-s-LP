Our Departments

Design

Development

Looking for a Frontend, Backend, Full stack developer, or QA tester vacancy? In our company, you will be engaged in the full support of the software development life cycle: analysis, design, development, testing, and implementation of clients and server-side of the website.

Marketing

Our company is waiting for specialists in SEO, SMM, PPC, Copywrite, Media buying, Email marketing, and Influence marketing fields. We require developing a content strategy, compiling reports on the performance of advertising campaigns, conducting research and analyzing trends, creating, editing, and updating content on the site or social networks.

Sales

Media

Virtual assistance
